Protocol Modules

Overview of the main smart-contract components in the protocol.

RWA Asset Token

ERC-20 token representing tokenized real-world assets with minting, burning, pausing, and role-based access control.

Asset Certificate NFT

ERC-721 certificate NFT used for asset proof metadata and permissioned certificate issuance.

Yield Vault

ERC-4626 vault where users can deposit RWA asset tokens and receive vault shares.

Oracle Adapter

Chainlink-style price adapter with stale price and invalid price protection.

Issuer Registry

Registry of authorized issuers allowed to mint and burn through the MintingManager.

Minting Manager

Connects issuers, asset tokens, and oracle validation for controlled asset issuance.

AMM

Constant-product automated market maker with liquidity provision, swaps, LP token minting, and reserve accounting.

Treasury

Stores ETH and ERC-20 funds with role-gated withdrawals and reentrancy protection.

Governance

Governor and timelock contracts for proposal creation, voting, queueing, and execution.
